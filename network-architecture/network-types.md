## Network Types (based on size)

- **Local Area Network (LAN):** Connects devices in a limited physical space, like a home, office, or school. Usually uses wired connections like Ethernet cables.

- **Wide Area Network (WAN):** Spans a larger geographical area, connecting LANs across cities, countries, or even continents. Often uses a combination of wired and wireless technologies.

- **Metropolitan Area Network (MAN):** Covers a larger area than a LAN, but smaller than a WAN, typically connecting LANs within a city or town.
